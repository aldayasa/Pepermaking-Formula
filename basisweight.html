Basis Weight Finder Calculator

Basis weight (or grammage) is the weight of paper per unit area, typically expressed in grams per square meter (GSM). It is a key quality parameter indicating the paper's thickness and density. In the paper industry, basis weight is crucial for quality control, production costs, and final product specifications.

Use this calculator to determine the basis weight (GSM) of your paper **before size press application**, based on the dry stock flow rate from the headbox, paper width, and machine speed.
